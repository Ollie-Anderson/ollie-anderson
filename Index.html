Bio

I am a developer and artist from the uk that specialises in truly bespoke software and full-package services. With great knowledge of the industry I can work with all teams to solve intriate problems, or build complete solutions ready to ship from the ground up. I am familiar with a variety of programming languages, software packages, and API Libraries that provide me with great flexibility and versatility in my work. My primary skills are in game development, where I excel in all aspects including programming; art; design-theory; and player-psychology. I also have strong skills in web development, team management, and art. So far, my projects have been Indie/Hobby focused, and I am looking to break into the industry. I have built this website to serve as a portfolio of my work.
